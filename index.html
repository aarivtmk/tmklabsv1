How to join?

It's a one week process.

01 - Day n

Register for the interview

The first step you need to do is fill the form with the accurate details. once you register, Kindly wait for a day to get the details.

02 - Day n

Get the Interview Schedule

Once you register, you will get a email from our team with the interview schedule details.
We never share any details on phone or Whatsapp. so kindly check your email for details.

03 - Day n+k

1 <= k <= 7

Attend Interview 1 and 2

The Interview session will be finished in 30 or 45 minutes. But in some cases, it can take a minimum of 2 days to maximum of 7 days to finish the 2 rounds.

04 - Day n+k

1 <= k <= 7

Interview 3 & Result

Once you finish the Interview 3, you will get the result in 24 hours.
If selected, collect the lab id and join the lab the next day. And in case you are not selected, you can reappear after 60 days.
